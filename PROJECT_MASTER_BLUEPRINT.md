# Saqr ERP - Master Blueprint

## Project Overview

Saqr ERP is an enterprise-grade ERP platform powered by Artificial Intelligence, designed to provide a scalable, modular, and high-performance business management system.

---

## Project Vision

To build a unified intelligent ERP system that integrates finance, inventory, HR, CRM, and AI-driven automation into a single ecosystem.

---

## Core Architecture Principles

- Modular Monolith / Microservices Ready
- Clean Architecture
- Domain-Driven Design (DDD)
- Scalable Multi-Tenant System
- API-First Design
- Event-Driven Communication

---

## Core Modules

- Authentication & Authorization
- User & Role Management
- Dashboard & Analytics
- Inventory Management
- Sales & POS
- Purchase Management
- Accounting & Finance
- Human Resources (HR)
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Supplier Management
- Asset Management
- Workflow Engine
- Document Management System (DMS)
- Audit & Compliance
- AI Automation Layer
- Reporting & Business Intelligence

## Technical Stack

### Frontend
- Flutter (Web / Mobile / Desktop)
- Material 3 Design System

### Backend
- ASP.NET Core 9 (REST + Clean Architecture)

### Database
- PostgreSQL (Primary DB)
- Redis (Caching / Sessions)

- Docker
